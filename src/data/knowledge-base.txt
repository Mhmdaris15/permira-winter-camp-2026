# Winter Camp 2026 Knowledge Base
# This file serves as the RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) context for the AI chatbot

## EVENT OVERVIEW
Winter Camp 2026 is an exciting 2-day winter cultural event organized by PERMIRA Saint Petersburg that brings together Indonesian students and participants from around the world for an unforgettable experience near the historic palaces of Saint Petersburg, Russia.

## EVENT DETAILS
- Event Name: Winter Camp 2026
- Dates: February 2-3, 2026
- Location: Pushkin (Tsarskoye Selo), Saint Petersburg, Russia
- Accommodation: Traditional Russian Dacha
- Duration: 2 days / 1 night
- Registration Fee: FREE
- Organizer: PERMIRA Saint Petersburg (Indonesian Student Association)
- Contact: @irazkisra (Telegram)

## ACCOMMODATION OPTIONS
The event offers dacha (traditional Russian cottage) accommodation in the Saint Petersburg area:

### Option 1: Strelna
- Location: Санкт-Петербург, Петродворцовый район, поселок Стрельна
- Near Konstantinovsky Palace
- Cost: 25,000-35,000₽/day (low season), 45,000-55,000₽/day (high season)
- Nearby: Большой Петергофский дворец (Grand Peterhof Palace)

### Option 2: Pushkin (Recommended)
- Location: Near Пулковское шоссе, Пушкинский район
- Address: пос. Александровская, ул. Встреч, 15
- Cost: ~50,000₽/day
- Nearby Museums:
  1. Екатерининский дворец (Catherine Palace) - г. Пушкин, ул. Садовая, д. 7
  2. Музей "Россия в великой войне" - г. Пушкин, Фермская дорога 5А

### Option 3: Chernaya Rechka
- Location: Санкт-Петербург, Приморский, Дибуновская улица, 32
- Cost: ~1,500,000₽/month
- Nearby: Эрмитаж старая деревня (Hermitage Old Village) - Заусадебная улица, дом 37 "В"

## ACTIVITIES INCLUDED

### Day 1 - Arrival & Cultural Exploration
- Arrival and check-in at the dacha
- Visit to Catherine Palace (Екатерининский дворец)
- Group photos at historic sites
- Welcome dinner with traditional Russian cuisine
- Evening bonfire gathering at the dacha

### Day 2 - Palace Tour & Cultural Exchange
- Visit to Peterhof Grand Palace (Большой Петергофский дворец)
- Photography sessions at winter palace gardens
- Cultural exchange activities
- Indonesian-Russian cultural sharing
- Farewell lunch and departure

## NEARBY ATTRACTIONS

### Palaces
1. Catherine Palace (Екатерининский дворец)
   - Address: г. Пушкин, ул. Садовая, д. 7
   - Famous for the Amber Room

2. Grand Peterhof Palace (Большой Петергофский дворец)
   - Address: г. Петергоф, ул. Разводная, д. 2
   - Known as the "Russian Versailles"

### Museums
1. Hermitage Old Village (Эрмитаж старая деревня)
   - Address: Заусадебная улица, дом 37 "В"
   - Storage facility with rotating exhibitions

2. "Russia in the Great War" Museum
   - Address: Санкт-Петербург, г. Пушкин, Фермская дорога 5А

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: Where is the event located?
A: The event takes place in Pushkin (Tsarskoye Selo) near Saint Petersburg, Russia, with dacha accommodation and visits to famous palaces like Catherine Palace and Peterhof.

Q: What is a dacha?
A: A dacha is a traditional Russian countryside cottage. We'll be staying at a comfortable dacha near the historic palaces of Saint Petersburg.

Q: What if I have food allergies?
A: Please specify all dietary restrictions and allergies in the registration form. We provide 100% halal options and accommodate all dietary needs including no pork/lard.

Q: Is the event really free?
A: Yes! Winter Camp 2026 is organized by PERMIRA for Indonesian students and interested participants. You only need to cover your travel costs to Saint Petersburg.

Q: What language is used?
A: English, Indonesian, and Russian are used. We have multilingual staff and participants.

Q: What will we visit?
A: The program includes visits to Catherine Palace (famous for the Amber Room), Peterhof Grand Palace (Russian Versailles), and potentially the Hermitage Old Village branch.

Q: How cold will it be?
A: February in Saint Petersburg can be cold (-5°C to -15°C). Please bring warm winter clothing, thermal underwear, and proper winter boots.

Q: How do I contact the organizer?
A: Contact @irazkisra on Telegram for any questions or registration confirmation.

Q: Is there transportation provided?
A: Bus transportation can be arranged through SPB-Bus company for palace visits and transfers.
