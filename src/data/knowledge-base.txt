## EVENT OVERVIEW
Winter Camp 2026 is an exciting 6-day winter adventure event that brings together participants from around the world for an unforgettable experience in the Swiss Alps.

## EVENT DETAILS
- Event Name: Winter Camp 2026
- Dates: January 15-20, 2026
- Location: Alpine Mountain Resort, Switzerland
- Duration: 6 days / 5 nights
- Registration Fee: FREE
- Target Audience: International students and young professionals

## ACTIVITIES INCLUDED

### Day 1 - Arrival & Welcome
- Airport pickup and transfer to resort
- Welcome ceremony and orientation
- Ice-breaker activities
- Welcome dinner with traditional Swiss cuisine

### Day 2-3 - Snow Sports
- Professional skiing lessons for all levels (beginner to advanced)
- Snowboarding lessons and workshops
- Cross-country skiing introduction
- Snow tubing fun activities

### Day 4 - Mountain Adventure
- Guided mountain hiking expeditions
- Winter survival workshops
- Snow shelter building
- Emergency preparedness training

### Day 5 - Creative & Team Building
- Snow photography sessions with professional guidance
- Team building activities and snow games
- Snowman building competition
- Winter art workshop

### Day 6 - Celebration & Departure
- Final group activities
- Award ceremony
- Farewell brunch
- Transfer to airport

## EVENING PROGRAMS
- Evening bonfire gatherings with hot cocoa and marshmallows
- Stargazing sessions (weather permitting)
- Cultural exchange nights
- Movie nights with winter-themed films
- Game nights and karaoke

## ACCOMMODATION
- Comfortable shared rooms at Alpine Mountain Resort
- Heating and hot water provided

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: Do I need skiing experience?
A: No! We welcome beginners and provide professional lessons for all skill levels.

Q: What if I have food allergies?
A: Please specify all dietary restrictions and allergies in the registration form. We accommodate all dietary needs.

Q: Is the event really free?
A: Yes! Winter Camp 2026 is completely free for selected participants. You only need to cover your travel costs to Switzerland.

Q: What language is used?
A: English is the main language of communication. However, we have multilingual staff who speak Indonesian, Russian, and other languages.

Q: Can I bring my own equipment?
A: Yes, you can bring your own ski/snowboard equipment if you prefer.

Q: What is the cancellation policy?
A: Please inform us at least 2 weeks before the event if you need to cancel. Last-minute cancellations may affect future applications.

Q: Is there medical support?
A: Yes, we have first aid trained staff on-site and the resort has a medical clinic nearby.

Q: What about COVID-19 measures?
A: We follow all current health guidelines. Please check for any travel requirements before your trip.
